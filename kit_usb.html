Frequently Asked Questions

What makes these "Special USB Kits" different from standard Live USBs?

Our Special USB Kits are highly curated solutions designed for specific advanced use cases like ethical hacking, comprehensive system recovery, secure offline environments, or running multiple OSes. They often include specialized software, detailed guides, and pre-configurations that go beyond a simple Live OS.

Can I use these kits on any computer?

These kits are designed for broad compatibility, supporting both Legacy BIOS and UEFI boot modes found in most modern and older PCs. As long as your computer can boot from a USB drive, our kits should work seamlessly.

Are the ethical hacking tools legal to use?

The ethical hacking tools in kits like "Hack In A Box" are intended strictly for educational purposes, personal learning, and testing your *own* networks and systems. Unauthorized use against any other network or system is illegal and unethical.

What is an "Offline Survival Box" used for?

The Offline Survival Box provides critical information and tools in scenarios without internet access. It can include essential software, manuals, troubleshooting guides, and educational content that's accessible anytime, anywhere, ensuring you're prepared for unexpected outages or remote work.

Can the "Dual OS Kit" install both operating systems on my computer?

The Dual OS Kit provides two operating systems on a single USB for portable use or installation. While it enables you to install both OSes to your computer's hard drive (creating a dual-boot setup), it's crucial to follow proper partitioning and installation procedures to avoid data loss.
