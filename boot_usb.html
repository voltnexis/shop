Frequently Asked Questions

Will this work on my PC?

Yes. Our bootable USBs are compatible with both **Legacy and UEFI** boot modes, ensuring they work on almost all modern and older PCs.

Do I need to configure anything?

No. Our USBs are pre-configured and tested. Just plug them in, select them as the boot device, and follow the on-screen installation steps.

This is an **Installer-Only** page. These products are designed for a clean installation and do not include a live boot environment. For live boot options, please check our Live USB Drives page.
