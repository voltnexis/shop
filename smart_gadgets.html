Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of a USB-C Multi Hub Adapter?

A USB-C Multi Hub Adapter expands the single USB-C port on your laptop or tablet into multiple ports, such as HDMI for external displays, additional USB-A ports for peripherals, and SD card readers, greatly increasing your device's versatility.

How does the VoltNexis NFC Business Card work?

Our NFC Business Card allows you to share your contact information, social media links, portfolio, or any custom URL by simply tapping the card on a compatible smartphone. It's a modern, eco-friendly, and highly efficient way to network.

Can the Pocket Mini Wi-Fi Router create a secure network?

Yes, the Pocket Mini Wi-Fi Router allows you to create your own secure, private Wi-Fi network from a wired internet connection (like in a hotel room) or extend an existing Wi-Fi signal, protecting your devices when using public Wi-Fi hotspots.
