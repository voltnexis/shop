Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I use a DVD instead of a USB?

DVDs are ideal for older systems that may not support USB booting, or when you need an unalterable, read-only medium for secure environments like exams or critical system recovery, as they cannot be easily modified after burning.

Are these DVDs compatible with all operating systems?

Our DVDs are designed to be universally bootable where an optical drive is present. The specific tools or OS on the DVD will function based on the intended purpose (e.g., recovery tools might be OS-agnostic, while an offline software DVD might be for Windows or Linux).

Can I install software from the Offline DVD?

Yes, the Offline DVD is preloaded with various software packages, drivers, and utilities that can be installed directly onto your system without requiring an internet connection. It's a great solution for new builds or systems without network access.
