Frequently Asked Questions

Will it work without installation?

Yes! Live USBs allow you to boot directly without installing anything on your hard drive. It's perfect for testing or temporary use.

Is persistence included?

You can choose USBs with or without persistence, depending on your storage needs. Persistence allows you to save files and settings on the USB drive.

How do I use it?

Just plug it into your PC, go into the BIOS/UEFI settings, and select the USB as your boot device. You'll be ready to go!
